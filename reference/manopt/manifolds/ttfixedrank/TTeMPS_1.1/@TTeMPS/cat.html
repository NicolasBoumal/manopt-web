cat

PURPOSE

CAT concatenation of two TT/MPS tensors.

SYNOPSIS

function z = cat( mu, x, y )

DESCRIPTION 

CAT concatenation of two TT/MPS tensors.
   Z = CAT(MU,X,Y) concatenates two TT/MPS tensors along the
   outer dimension MU. 
   The rank of the resulting tensor is rank(X) + rank(Y).

   See also PLUS, MINUS.
