plus

PURPOSE

PLUS Addition of two TT/MPS tensors.

SYNOPSIS

function z = plus( x, y )

DESCRIPTION 

PLUS Addition of two TT/MPS tensors.
   Z = PLUS(X,Y) adds two TT/MPS tensors. The rank of the resulting
   tensor is 2*R.

   See also MINUS, UMINUS.
