plus

PURPOSE

PLUS Addition of two TT/MPS operators.

SYNOPSIS

function z = plus( x, y )

DESCRIPTION 

PLUS Addition of two TT/MPS operators.
   Z = PLUS(X,Y) adds to TT/MPS operators. The resulting TT/MPS operator 
   has rank equal to the sum of the individual ranks.
